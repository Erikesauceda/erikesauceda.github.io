The Challenge

Clapper needed to grow organic discoverability in a market dominated by TikTok and Instagram Reels — without a paid acquisition budget. Every download had to be earned through the store.

My Approach

Built a repeatable ASO system from scratch — keyword research and prioritization, metadata optimization across iOS and Android, creative asset strategy (screenshots, videos, icons), regional localization for U.S. and LATAM, and continuous A/B testing. Tracked LTV and retention signals to ensure high-quality user acquisition.

The Project

DayGlass is an offline-first journaling app designed for calm, distraction-free writing. Built solo under ZenStudios — I owned every dimension from concept through dual platform launch on Google Play and the iOS App Store.

What I Owned

All product decisions, feature prioritization, UX architecture, and visual design. Full ASO strategy for both stores including keyword clustering and metadata optimization. In-app billing — purchase flows, entitlement logic, and freemium-to-paid conversion model. Go-to-market strategy built around organic discoverability from day one.

Key Decisions

Privacy-first and fully offline by design — no ads, no data collection, no account required. A one-time purchase Pro tier designed to build trust with privacy-conscious users. Distinctive glassmorphic aesthetic to stand out in a crowded journaling category.

The Platform

Cita was a peer-to-peer marketplace for buying and selling reservations at exclusive, hard-to-book dining locations — serving nearly 40,000 users with ~18,000 MAU and 2,100 DAU.

My Approach

Led lifecycle growth across email, push, SMS, social, and paid channels. Developed segmented campaigns by cuisine interest and booking behavior — targeting last-minute bookers and long-horizon planners separately. Continuous A/B testing across messaging, creative, and timing.
